What does MadSwag do for my business?

Make sports brackets fun and easy. We set up the games, collect and grade the picks, and notify the winners for you, so that you can focus on your business.

Reward repeat customers. Winners simply turn in a receipt from a prior visit in order to claim their prize. No punch cards or special equipment is necessary.

Advertise your specials, menu items, and events. Like a mailing list people actually read! When you join our network, you can place targeted, customizable ads in our e-mails to local players at no extra charge.

Give you free promo materials and useful analytics. Our table tents, flyers, and giveaways will help you get your customers excited about the game, and our data and analytics will help you make the most of it as it runs.

Customize your game. Do you want to award gift certificates to the top three players? Give each of the top five players a free pitcher of beer? Offer a single grand prize of free nachos for a month? You can set the prize structure the way that you want it, and you can even change it up week by week to suit your needs.

Build an online community for you. We put together a unique page for your business, help you drive traffic to your website, Facebook page, and Twitter feed, and use Facebook Comments and public leaderboards to fuel debate and competition between groups of friends and local customers.

Make game day more fun. Your customers aren't just watching the game on TV anymore. They're competing with their friends for bragging rights and fun prizes that keep them coming back to your business.
